hello! my name is marisa kahn.


Hi! I'm Marisa Kahn, an aspiring artist open to exploring all that graphic design has to offer. I fell in love with the world of graphic design through an elective class I took in eighth grade, and I have been pursuing it ever since. In the summer of 2021, I took a graphic design class that introduced me to basics in Adobe Photoshop. By taking digital media classes throughout high school, I further developed my knowledge and personal style within graphic design. Currently, I am attending San Francisco State University and studying Visual Communication Design and Psychology with hopes of growing my skills even further. My goal in life is to make a positive impact by helping others. I am very interested in creating design that is inclusive of and accessible to all, particularly in reference to UX design.
